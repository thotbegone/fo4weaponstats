Fallout 4 Weapon Stats

Please note, some weapons add radiation or elemental damage. This will be listed in the description at some point. All damage is combined for now, save for radiation, since it doesn't deal direct damage.

Weapon files take a while to put together, due to all the mods having different effects on every weapon. Since there is no consistency, everything is manual. I'm in the process of updating files so that there will be damage type fields for all weapons.

If anyone wants a specific weapon added, email me at fo4stats@gmail.com.
